# HAMS Research Folder

Collected from official PAKHAMS/PARS pages on 2026-05-20.

## Start Here

- `guide.html` - self-contained browsable guide covering HAM/SWL basics, legal licensing, PTA rules, contacts, and embedded downloaded PDFs/resources.
- `pakhams-research.md` - readable brief covering what PARS/PAKHAMS is, SWL vs HAM, and the registration path.
- `contacts.json` - official body, regional coordinators, emails, phone numbers, and mailing address from the site.
- `forms-index.json` - downloaded form inventory with source URLs.
- `members.csv` - official PARS members table scraped from the PAKHAMS members page.

## Downloaded Forms

- `forms/pakhams/` - PAKHAMS SWL membership forms uploaded on the official site.
- `forms/pta/` - PTA license ZIP downloads linked by the PAKHAMS membership page.
- `forms/pta/extracted/` - extracted PTA PDFs.
- `text/` - searchable text extracted from all downloaded PDFs.

## Raw Official Pages

The `raw/` folder keeps local HTML/JSON copies of the pages used as sources: home, become-member, contact, executive body, core values, members, and WordPress media JSON.
