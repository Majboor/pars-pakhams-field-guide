2. Application Process.
a) Application (2 complete sets) shall be forwarded to the Director (RBS), PTA HQs
on the prescribed application form along with the required documents.
b) In case of any deficiency, RBS Directorate will either inform/liaise on with
applicant within 7 working days for the same or return the application in order to
fulfil the deficiencies and resubmit, depending on the level of deficiency or
forward onward.
c) If the application is found complete:
i.

the same shall be forwarded to LEAs for applicant’s security clearance.
-3-

ii.

the applicant shall be informed about the date and time for written test
and interview, which will be conducted by PTA at either its HQs or
Zonal Offices. However, an exemption from written test and interview
can be granted by the Authority, on case to case basis of qualified
applicants on production of experience certificate from relevant forum.

iii.

Applicant has to clear the tests within maximum three (03) attempts. In
case of failure in Three (03) attempts, the applicant has to re-apply.

d) LEAs will clear the applicant from security point of view and forward its response
to PTA within Twelve (12) weeks.
e) If LEAs reject the application, it shall document the reason, for record of PTA only.
The applicant shall be informed accordingly and case will be considered closed.
f) In case of no response from LEAs within stipulated time (12 weeks), the case shall
be processed for award of provisional permission.
3. Issuance of Provisional Permission/ Amateur License.
a) If the applicant qualifies written test and interview, a Demand note (DN) will be
issued to the applicant for fee deposit within 20 days of its issuance.
b) In case of failure to pay the requisite dues, a reminder shall be given to the
applicant, to clear the dues within one week. Any failure in payment to the
extension shall be considered, that applicant is not interested in the license and
hence case shall be considered closed.
c) Upon receipt of payment, and confirmation of fee deposit by Finance Division,
RBS Directorate will process the case for approval of the Authority, for award of
provisional Permission.
d) After the Authority Approval, the Provisional Permission shall be issued.
e) The provisional permission shall be cancelled immediately, if the case is not
approved by LEAs.
f) On receipt of Clearance from LEAs, RBS will prepare the details of licensee and
forward the same for printing of the Amateur License Card. The same will be
issued after printing.
g) Applicant from age 16 to less than 18 years shall be issued provisional permission
with lower power level of 10 Watts maximum, provided that the
supervisor/guardian of the applicant is an amateur licensee having the license for

the same location as applied by the applicant. If the applicant is between age16-18

4. Examination.
The prescribed written test and interview, from Syllabus and other details as given
in Annexure-II, shall be conducted at PTA HQs or Zonal Offices, in the presence
of PARS member(if available), on the dates notified to the applicant.
Minimum passing marks for test and interview are 50%.
5. Applicable Fee.
a) The examination fee of Rs. 100/- be submitted prior to examination.
b) The license shall be issued on payment of initial license fee of Rs.1,000/- for five
(05) years. Similarly, Rs.1,000/- will also be charged as renewal fee for a period of
next five (05)years.
c) All the payments be made in the form of Demand Draft/ pay order only, in the
name of “Pakistan Telecommunication Authority and addressed to Director (RBS).
d) No refund of the fee under any circumstances will be applicable.
e) All the payments are subject to the revision by PTA.
6. Renewal of License.–
a) The license may be renewed for a maximum period of five (05) years, after making
due payments.
b) If a license is not renewed before its expiry, and such individual requests PTA for
renewal of the license at a later date, then fresh application on prescribed
application form may be submitted and such case shall be exempted from security
clearance and written test/ interview.
c) Senior Citizens, age 60 years and above, shall be given life time renewals.
7. Termination/Cancellation of License.
The license shall terminate in the following situations:
a) On the death of licensee;
b) If the licensee turns insane or incapable of running amateur station;

c) When the licensee being found guilty of any criminal offence, malpractice or moral
turpitude, as decided by the Authority; and/or
d) On non-payment of renewal fee within stipulated time as per issued Demand
Note.
8. Change of Site Location.
The licensee shall not be allowed to shift the station to a location other than from the
address given on the license in the same city or otherwise without prior approval of
the Authority. The request for change of location to the Authority shall be forwarded
with the following information:
a) The date and reason for the shifting of the location; and
b) The exact new geographical location of station.
c) A revised License shall be issued after internal approval and on payment of Rs.
200/9. Issuance of duplicate license.
a) The licensee shall inform Director RBS in writing, in case the issued license is lost,
mutilated or destroyed.
b) If the concerned DG of RBS Directorate is satisfied with the explanation, a
duplicate license shall be issued on payment of Rs.200/- .
c) All Payments for the application/renewal of the wireless license shall be made in
the form of Demand Draft, pay order only in the name of “Pakistan
Telecommunication Authority”. Original receipt must accompany along with the
application to process the case.
10. Mobile or Portable Station ( Special Mobile Authorization)
a)

Use of Mobile or portable station in Pakistan is not allowed,

b) However, the Authority may issue special authorization to establish, maintain
and operate an amateur radio station as a portable/mobile station , for specific
period of time, on special occasions like exhibition, conference or for special
technical investigations or in emergency situation like floods, earthquake or any
natural calamity, on case to case basis, provided;
i.

Application for such authorization is made fifteen (15) days in advance,
however, in emergency situation like floods, earthquake or any natural
calamity application 15 days in advance will not be applied to grant mobile

the licensees from academia to conduct experiment(s), subject to

experiment(s).

d) the special authorization for portable/mobile operation may be issued to the

a period no more than thirty (30) days , extendable once, on request, for

iii.

iv.

other conditions as the Authority may determine from time to time.

The Authority reserves the rights to revoke the Authorization any time, without
assigning any reason.
